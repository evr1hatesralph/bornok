How to Measure Your Bust

Wrap a measuring tape around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Ensure the tape is not too tight and you can breathe comfortably.

How to Measure Your Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 7-9 inches below your natural waistline. Make sure the tape is straight and parallel to the floor all the way around your body.

Size Conversion Note

Our sizes are designed to be true to size, but sizing can vary depending on design and materials. If you're unsure about your size or fall between sizes, we recommend sizing up. For precise measurements, follow our measurement guide above.
