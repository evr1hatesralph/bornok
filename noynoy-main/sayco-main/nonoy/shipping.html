Shipping & Returns

Note: Due to the current global situation, shipping times may be longer than usual. We appreciate your patience and understanding.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached
  • Swimwear, underwear, and jewelry can only be returned if the hygiene seal is unbroken
  • Sale items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged
  • Items purchased with a promotional discount may be refunded at the discounted value

How to Return an Item

  1. Log in to your account and go to your order history, or find your order confirmation email
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return and the reason for your return
  3. Print the prepaid return shipping label (for domestic returns) or arrange your own return shipping (for international returns)
  4. Package your items securely in their original packaging if possible
  5. Attach the shipping label and drop off the package at the designated shipping carrier

Processing Time: Returns are typically processed within 5-7 business days of receipt. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the quickest processing time and availability of your preferred item.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and packaging. We'll arrange for a return or replacement at no cost to you.
